# Stakeholder Presentation Script: Resume Engine AI

**Target Duration**: 3 Minutes (Approx. 420 words)
**Tone**: Professional, Innovative, and Data-Driven

---

## [0:00 - 0:40] The Problem: The High Cost of Manual Screening

"Every year, companies lose thousands of hours and millions in revenue due to one simple fact: **manual resume screening is broken.**

Recruiters are drowning in a sea of applications, spending an average of just **6 seconds** per resume. This leads to massive burnout and, more importantly, **massive bias.** Traditional ATS systems filter by rigid keywords, often missing high-potential candidates—especially students and career-shifters—simply because they don't have the 'right' enterprise buzzwords. This 'talent blindness' is why teams struggle to find the technical depth they need, despite a record number of applicants."

## [0:40 - 1:10] The Solution: Introducing Resume Engine AI

"Good morning everyone. Today, I’m excited to present **Resume Engine AI**—a next-generation candidate evaluation platform designed to bridge this gap. We aren't just another filtering tool; we are an intelligence layer that understands **technical potential** as deeply as a senior engineer would."

---

## [1:10 - 1:50] Core Pillar 1: Intelligent LLM Extraction

"At the heart of our engine is a sophisticated **Multi-Stage Semantic Parser**. Unlike legacy tools that rely on regex patterns, we use Large Language Models—including OpenAI and local Ollama instances—to extract structured data with human-level context.

When a resume says 'Built a RAG pipeline,' our engine doesn't just see a project. It automatically infers expertise in **Vector Databases** and **LLM Orchestration**. We’ve implemented cascading extraction for complex PDF layouts, ensuring that no achievement is lost in translation, regardless of the document's formatting."

---

## [1:50 - 2:30] Core Pillar 2: The 'Student-Aware' Scoring Advantage

"Our most innovative feature is our **Adaptive Scoring Algorithm**. Most ATS systems use a one-size-fits-all model that favors years of experience above all else. 

We’ve pioneered a **Fresher-Aware Mode**. For students and early-career talent, our algorithm automatically shifts weight. Instead of penalizing for zero years of 'full-time' work, it intensifies the focus on **Technical Projects (20%)** and **Skill Richness (30%)**. This allows recruiters to identify high-potential 'diamonds in the rough' who would otherwise be filtered out by traditional criteria."

---

## [2:30 - 2:50] Core Pillar 3: Profile Enrichment & ROI

"Finally, we prioritize operational transparency and verification. Our engine integrates directly with **GitHub and LeetCode** to verify technical claims, while our **Langfuse Observability integration** tracks every AI decision. 

We monitor token consumption, latency, and estimated costs in real-time. We can prove the ROI by showing exactly how much faster we move candidates through the pipeline while keeping infrastructure costs optimized."

---

## [2:50 - 3:00] Conclusion

"In summary, Resume Engine AI isn't just a parser. It’s a precision tool for identifying the right talent, at the right time, while ensuring fairness and transparency. 

Thank you, and I’m happy to take any questions."
